Meta Ad Performance Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard built to analyze and monitor Meta advertising performance across Facebook and Instagram, helping marketing teams make faster and smarter decisions on campaign spend and audience targeting.

---

Tech Stack Used

Power BI Desktop — dashboard building and visualizations
DAX — custom measures and KPI calculations
Power Query — data cleaning and transformation
Excel / CSV — source data format

---

Data Source

The dataset contains Meta ad campaign data including impressions, clicks, engagements, purchases, budget, and audience demographics broken down by age, gender, country, ad type, and time period.

---

Features and Highlights

Business Problem

Marketing teams running ads on Facebook and Instagram often struggle to understand which campaigns, ad formats, and audiences are actually driving results. Without a centralized view, budget gets wasted on underperforming ads and opportunities are missed.

Goal of the Dashboard

The goal was to build a single interactive dashboard that gives a clear picture of ad performance across both platforms, so decision makers can quickly identify what is working, who is engaging, and where to shift budget for better returns.

Key Visuals and Why They Were Used

KPI Cards at the top — Impressions, Clicks, CTR, Engagement Rate, Conversion Rate, Purchases, and Total Budget are shown as headline numbers so stakeholders get an instant snapshot without digging into charts.

Engagements by Gender — A donut chart to quickly identify whether male or female audiences are responding better, useful for refining targeting strategy.

Engagements by Age — A bar chart showing which age groups engage most, helping the team decide where to concentrate ad spend by demographic.

Weekly Engagements Trend — A bar chart tracking week over week performance to spot momentum, drops, or seasonal patterns in campaign engagement.

Hourly Engagements Trend — A line chart showing engagement by hour of the day, used to optimize ad scheduling for peak audience activity.

Engagements by Country — A map visual to understand geographic reach and identify high performing or untapped regions.

Analysis by Month — A calendar heatmap showing which days of the week perform best, helping plan future campaign schedules.

Analysis by Ad Type — A table breaking down Stories, Image, Carousel, and Video ads by impressions, clicks, CTR, purchase rate, engagement rate, and conversion rate, so teams can compare format performance side by side.

Platform Toggle — A switcher between Facebook and Instagram so the same dashboard serves both platforms without needing separate reports.

----
Business Impacts and Insights

Carousel ads delivered the strongest engagement rate despite Stories having higher impressions, suggesting budget should shift toward Carousel format for better ROI.

The 25 to 34 age group was the highest engaging demographic across both platforms, making it the priority target segment for future campaigns.

Engagement consistently peaked mid-week from Tuesday to Thursday, indicating the best days to schedule high-budget campaigns.

Instagram and Facebook showed similar conversion rates despite different CTRs, meaning both platforms are worth investing in but for different objectives.

Female audiences made up around 43% of total Facebook engagements, pointing to stronger resonance with female targeted creatives.
